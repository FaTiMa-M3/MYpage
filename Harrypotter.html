HARRY POTTER

Harry Potter, also known as the boy who lived, is the main character of the story. He is described as having black, scruffy hair,
green eyes and a thin lightning bolt scar on his forehead. His scar came from when Voldemort tried to kill him. He descovers on his 11 birthday he is a wizard, famous for his scar and navigates the Wizarding world as a celebrity.
Harry lives with his aunt Petunia Dursley, uncle Vernon Dursley and cousin Dudley in number 4 privet drive. None of them are magical or nice to Harry at all. They hate Harry because he has magic and punish him for it. Regardless, Harry persists and manages to attend Hogwarts school of witchcraft and wizardry, despite all the Dursleys efforts. He always has a grand time there and misses it dearly when it's time to return.
