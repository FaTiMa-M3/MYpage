HARRY POTTER

Harry Potter, also known as the boy who lived, is the main character of the story. He is described as having black, scruffy hair,
green eyes and a thin lightning bolt scar on his forehead. His scar came from when Voldemort tried to kill him. He descovers on his 11 birthday
he is a wizard, famous for his scar and navigates the Wizarding world as a celebrity.
Harry lives with his aunt Petunia Dursley, uncle Vernon Dursley and cousin Dudley in number 4 privet drive. None of them are magical or nice to
Harry at all. They hate Harry because he has magic and punish him for it. Regardless, Harry persists and manages to attend Hogwarts school of witchcraft
and wizardry, despite all the Dursleys efforts. He always has a grand time there and misses it dearly when it's time to return.

Rating: 8/10

Harry gets an 8/10 because he is an amazing character. He is humble, loyal, funny, relatable and overall intresting to read about. I enjoy reading as
he learns more about The Wizarding world and his destiny. His flaws are what made him feel human and relatable but, it was quite annoying that he
never try to overcome them. One example is his impulsiveness. He would launch himself into random situations with no plan of what to do. One example
is when he goes to hogwarts to find a horcrux in the deathly hallows. He has no idea where this horcrux is, what it is and how to destroy it but
still does this act. In my opinion him finding it was sheer luck and it could've gone the wrong way easily. To sum it up, while I wish the books
focused on Harry fixing his flaws, he still has great qualities themake him a great main character.
