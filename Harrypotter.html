HARRY POTTER


"I’ll be in my bedroom, making no noise and pretending I dont exist." — Harry Potter

Harry Potter, also known as the boy who lived, is the main character of the story. He descovers on his 11 birthday he is a wizard, famous for his scar
and navigates the Wizarding world as a celebrity. Harry lives with his aunt Petunia Dursley, uncle Vernon Dursley and cousin Dudley in number 4
privet drive. None of them are magical or nice to Harry at all. They hate Harry because he has magic and punish him for it. Regardless, Harry persists
and manages to attend Hogwarts school of witchcraft and wizardry, despite all the Dursleys efforts. He always has a grand time there and misses it dearly
when it's time to return back to the muggle world.

Rating: 8/10

Harry gets an 8/10 because while he is a good mix of good and bad none of his flaws are properly show up for him to overcome. Some of his flaws are:
  • He is emotionally immature and weak which makes him very impulsive (His lash out to Seamus, plans to kill Sirius and his early rage
    towards his friends in Order of the Pheonix)
  • He doesn't trust others and feels the need to take things into his own hands (He flew to the department of mysteries
    without listening to anyone around him and with no plan to save Sirius)
  • When he believes he is without a doubt correct, Harry can be very self-righteous (He was very closed-minded when he made the theory about
    the Deathly Hallows on little to no eveidence at all)
These flaws are the main cause for heaps of trouble that could've been avoided. If he had listened to his friends and tried to keep a level-head,
the Battle of the Department of Mysteries may have not been so disatrous. His occainsional self-righteous and arrogant attitude is never addressed
and his impulsive rage leads him to make stupid choices.

He still has good qualities that out-weigh his bad ones. His main ones are:
  • Bravery (Snuck into the Ministry of Magic, formed the DA even though it was banned)
  • He will sacrifice himself, if it will saves others (He willingly let Voldemort attempt to kill him in Deathly Hallows)
  • He is loyal. Harry never leaves his friends and showed genuine friendship to all (His friendship with Ron and Hermione)
  • He forgives people. At the end of the series, Harry has no long-time grudges towards anyone except Voldemort, not Percy, Draco or even Snape
Harry is overall a good character, who does his best to help others. He never stands down and teaches us the importance of love, friendship and
standing up for what is right even if you stand alone.
