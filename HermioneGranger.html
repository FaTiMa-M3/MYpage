Hermione Granger


“Honestly, don’t you two read?!” - Hermione Granger

Hermione Granger, the feminist icon, the book-smart queen and the main reason Harry and Ron weren't killed or worse expelled. She is an icon
and a legend. She is a part of the Golden Trio and one of Harry's best friends. I enjoy seeing how she quickly uses her intelligence and strength
and her character development throughout the series.

Rating:7/10

Hermione gets a 7/10 because while I love her she has some traits which bug me to no end. Her bad traits that annoy me the most are:
  • Her absolute belief she is right and how she doesn't admit when she's wrong (her attitude towards the firebolt, crookshanks and Trelawnwy)
  • Her need to be the best (The Half-blood prince book saga)
  • She does what shebelieves is best for other, not what is actually good (S.P.E.W)
  • She is vindictive and has extremely violent burst of anger (Set birds on Ron, punched Draco and also cursed the DADA poster to leave lasting
    scars on traitors)
  • Perfectionist. Hermione has a clear fear of being a failure. (Her boggart turns into Professor Mcgonagall telling her she failed and her
    reaction to her OWL results)
There are many more and few are actually mentioned in the books, leaving the rest as if they didn't exist. It is almost as if J.K Rowling made Hermione
her perfect character and pushes down her flaws. It is really annoying to hear mostly that Hermione is smart and powerful and kind when I would've
liked an arc where she accepts she isn't perfect and it's ok to fail. But instead we get a perfectionist, vindictive hero who must always be right.

Not to say Hermione isn't without her good qualities. She isn't just intelligent and powerful but also determined, hard-working and compassionate.
It may be hard to see it but she is more then just smart. In the Deathly Hallows, she was the one to comfort Harry when at his parents grave. To
save both of them from Nagini. To heal Ron when he was injured. To put the blame on herself when Harry and Ron saved her from a mountain troll.
To protect her parents by making them forget who she was. She truly cares about all her friends and family and goes to massive legnths to protect them.
