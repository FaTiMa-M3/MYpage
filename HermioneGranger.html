Hermione Granger


“Honestly, don’t you two read?!” - Hermione Granger

Hermione Granger, the feminist icon, the book-smart queen and the main reason Harry and Ron weren't killed or worse expelled. She is an icon
and a legend. She is a part of the Golden Trio and one of Harry's best friends. I enjoy reading about how she quickly uses her intelligence and
strength and her character development.

Rating:7/10
